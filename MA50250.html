Reading

Please select one of the following topics and prepare a 15 minute presentation. Within some of these topics, I have included a selection of (related) papers. You may do the presentation on just one of these papers or several.

  • Maximum entropy regularisation

    • Convergence rates for maximum entropy regularisation by Engl and Landl

    • Regularization of ill-posed problems with non-negative solutions by Clason et al

  • Iterative regularisation

    • An Iterative Regularization Method for Total Variation-Based Image Restoration by Osher et al

    • Iterative Total Variation Schemes for Nonlinear Inverse problems by Bachmayr and Burger

  • Data assimilation with Lagrangian data

    • A method for assimilation of Lagrangian data by Kuznetsov et al

    • A Method for Assimilating Lagrangian Data into a Shallow-Water-Equation Ocean Model by Salman et al

    • Variational assimilation of Lagrangian data in in oceanography by Nodel

  • Recovery sparse spikes (via a continuous version of l1 regularisation)

    • Spike detection from inaccurate samplings by Azais et al

    • Towards a Mathematical Theory of Super-Resolution by Candes and Fernandez-Granda
